About the Conference

A Premier Platform for
Future Diplomats

NNRG Model United Nations 2026 brings together brilliant minds from across the nation to engage in meaningful diplomatic discourse.

The NNRG Model United Nations Conference represents a unique convergence of academic excellence and practical diplomatic training. Organized by the prestigious Nalla Narasimha Reddy Education Society's Group of Institutions, this conference is designed to cultivate the next generation of global leaders.

Through intensive committee sessions, expert-led workshops, and real-world simulation of United Nations proceedings, delegates will develop critical skills in negotiation, public speaking, research, and consensus-building.
